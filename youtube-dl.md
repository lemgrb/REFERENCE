## Merged audio and video

> youtube-dl -f bestvideo[ext=mp4]+bestaudio[ext=m4a]/mp4 ${URL}

## Audio mp3

> youtube-dl --extract-audio --audio-format mp3 <video URL> [-a, --batch-file FILE]
